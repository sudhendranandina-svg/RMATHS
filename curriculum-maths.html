Frequently Asked Questions

How do you align with my child's school curriculum?

We request your child's current math textbooks and school curriculum outline to ensure our lessons directly support their classroom learning.

Can you help with homework and assignments?

Yes! We provide homework support and help with specific assignments while teaching the underlying concepts for long-term understanding.

What if my child is ahead of their year level?

We offer extension programs for gifted students, introducing advanced concepts while ensuring they remain engaged and challenged.

How do you communicate progress to parents?

We provide regular progress reports and maintain open communication with parents about their child's development and areas of focus.

Do you prepare students for school assessments?

Absolutely! We help students prepare for school tests, PAT assessments, and other evaluations with targeted practice and test strategies.

What's the difference between this and other math tutoring?

Our approach combines curriculum alignment with proven teaching methods from abacus and Vedic math, creating a comprehensive learning experience.
