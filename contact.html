Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a free trial class?

Simply fill out the contact form above, call us at +64 21 083 50210, or send an email to abucasaspirants@gmail.com. We'll schedule a convenient time for your child's trial class.

What happens during the trial class?

The trial class includes an assessment of your child's current math level, introduction to our teaching methods, and a sample lesson. You'll receive feedback and a personalized learning plan.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We offer both online and in-person classes. Our online classes are interactive and engaging, with small group sizes to ensure personalized attention.

What are your class schedules?

We offer flexible scheduling including weekday afternoons, evenings, and weekend sessions. We work with your family's schedule to find convenient times.

How long are the classes?

Regular classes are typically 45-60 minutes for group sessions and 60 minutes for one-on-one curriculum support. Trial classes are 45 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, online payments, and cash for in-person classes. Payment plans are available for longer courses.
